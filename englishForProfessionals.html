English for Professionals

English for your profession, your job, your project.

English for Professional was an idea that came up after a special request from a professional. He needed to learn English from the beginning, and start reading material at work. This is how we came out with a special program of studies designed to fulfil his needs.

How we Work

You just need to ask for a course and tell us about your special needs and how much you know of English. We will design the course according to this and you will start reading English texts from the first class.

This is an online course, with videos explaining each topic, a forum to contact your teacher at any time, homeworks to practice what you learn, materials related to your field of work, a glosary to complete with your mates, and tests see your progress.
