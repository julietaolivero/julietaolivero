English Mates

English for Children

English Mates is a project that was created out of the idea that children are capable of more things than the regular goals they have to achieve in educational environments. Also, we believe in the idea that a new approach to teaching, one more technology-oriented is needed to catch their attention, to help them understand abstract concepts, and to give children the chance to develop new skills, such as documentation, dictionary work, internet use, self-learning and problem solving.

The Classes

We work with small amounts of children, to give them a personalized attention, and the possibility to enjoy each and every one of the games and activities. It is destined to childrem from the age of 7 who are willing to learn a new language together with the culture it represents.

The course is adapted to the specific needs of the students. As regards resources, it is wide and varied to provide students with different kinds of activities and approaches. Of course, videos, computer games, online search and all kind of technological activities are included, such as video and audio recording and multimedia tasks. Children not only use the computer to listen and watch. They use it to write, speak and solve problems. New characters and trendy cartoons are a must in our classes, since they help students learn quickly and un a fun way.
