Julieta Soledad Olivero

English > Spanish translator

My name is Julieta Olivero. I am an English into Spanish translator. I studied in the National University of Cordoba, a prestigious institution in Latin America. I work as a freelance translator. My areas of expertise are science, technology, medicine, and commerce. I am also interesting in subtitling, website localization and software localization. I am also interesting in giving and receiving education. I attend courses and participate in congresses all the time. I consider that to be an excellent way open my mind to new points of view. I also work as an English teacher. I teach children from primary school. I am prepared and willing to solve problems and to answer questions.

Freelance Translator

I consider constant training to be the key for any professional to enhance their abilities and give clients a high quality service. I am continuously attending courses, congresses and seminars to meet other professionals, know their experiences and take advice that will be useful for my professional career. I have participated in courses and congresses on phonetics and phonology, sociolinguistics and discourse analysis, CAT tools, translation strategies, Spanish, English, text editing and software and hardware for translator. ProZ.com seminars are also a good way of learning and meeting colleagues.

Work Experience

I have worked in a variety of projects, mainly from the field of agriculture and machinery. I consider reliability the most important characteristic of a professional. As a language services provider, my priorities are providing my client with high quality services and complying with the arrangements made. I am here to give solutions to my clients. I also work as a volunteer translator for different non-profit organizations. I am currently working with translationsforprogress.org and amara.org. I consider this both a way to help organizations achieve their objectives, and a way to gain experience in the fields I am interested in

About Shift

Since 2013, we have successfully worked and comply with this rules, and the results were awesome.
